Breakfast

Breakfast is probably the third best meal on weekdays, and the second best on weekends.
On weekdays, its mostly rushed. You grab something so you won't be hungry all day at school or work.
Usually toast or cereal or something not that interesting.
It's better if its a weekend. Then you have more time to actually make something.
